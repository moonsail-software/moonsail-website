Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 20-20-20 rule?

It is a common eye-strain habit: every 20 minutes, look at something at least 20 feet away for 20 seconds to give your eyes a chance to relax.

Who is this app for?

Eye Break is designed for people who spend long stretches on screens, especially developers, students, office workers, and anyone doing focused digital work.

Does the app keep running in the background?

Yes. The app uses notifications and a foreground service so your reminders can stay on schedule while you use other apps.
