Mix – Concrete Calculator

Calculate concrete mix ratios, bag counts, and volume for five shapes — fast and free. No subscription, no account required.

Get it on Google Play

What it calculates

Mix handles five shape types on one screen: rectangular slab, round slab, footing, column, and steps. Enter dimensions and results appear instantly. Whether you're pouring a driveway pad, a round column base, a foundation footing, or a concrete staircase, it's all in one place.

Steps calculations are a genuine gap in free concrete calculator apps — most stop at slabs and footings. Mix includes them, so you can estimate a staircase pour like anything else.

Concrete mix calculator

Mix is a full concrete mix design calculator, not just a volume tool. Select a mix grade from M10 to M50, or enter a custom mix ratio for your specific job. For each grade, you get the complete material breakdown: cement, sand, gravel, and water — calculated for the exact volume you're pouring.

Bag count is calculated for your pour alongside the full material breakdown. Enter a price per cubic yard and Mix adds an estimated cost to your results.

How to use it

Select a shape. Enter your dimensions — a labeled diagram shows which measurement goes where, so there's no guessing which field is "length" vs. "depth." Choose a mix grade or type in a custom ratio. Set your waste factor (default 10%) and an optional price per cubic yard. Tap Calculate to see your results. Save any calculation to your history and reload it from the saved calculations sheet.

Units

Mix works in both imperial and metric. Imperial outputs concrete volume in cubic yards and cubic feet. Metric outputs cubic meters and uses kg bag sizes. Switch between them with a single tap at the top of the screen.
