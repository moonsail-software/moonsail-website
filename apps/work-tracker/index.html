Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Work Hours Tracker for?

It is designed for gig drivers, hourly freelancers, and anyone who wants a simple work log that goes beyond raw hours and shows real take-home value after fuel.

Do I need to create an account?

No. The app is local-first and keeps your shift records on your device, so there is no signup, cloud dashboard, or subscription wall.

Is GPS required?

No. GPS mileage tracking is optional. If you prefer a more private setup, odometer mode lets you track distance without location permission.

Is the app available now?

Yes. Work Hours Tracker is live on Google Play for Android.
